About Us

Welcome to Dawa Mashinani, an application dedicated to safeguarding Kenya's agricultural heritage by effectively managing and mitigating the impact of pests and crop diseases.
Our mission is to ensure that the Kenyan government—specifically the Ministry of Agriculture—receives timely reports about various pest and crop disease infestations from across the country, allowing for mitigation before the situation worsens. These reports, provided by agricultural extension officers situated in different parts of the country, ensure that every farmer, no matter where they are, has equal access to the resources and support needed to protect their crops and livelihoods.

Contact Us

Farmers assist by contacting us to inform us of pests or crop diseases observed in their areas, especially when agricultural officers have not yet managed to reach their location.
